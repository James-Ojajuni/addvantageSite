Is the power grid in Zanzibar/Tanzania and Fumba Town stable?

Many digital nomads and internet freelancers live and work in the city. To ensure a reliable power supply, Fumba Town offers an automatic backup power system that activates during outages. This optional service can be added for just $0.09 per sqm per month.

For a 50sqm apartment, the additional cost for automatic backup power is only $4.50 per month.

Is the internet in Zanzibar and Fumba Town stable and fast enough for remote work?

In Fumba Town, Zanzibar, every apartment and house is equipped with a fiber-optic connection, ensuring fast and highly reliable internet. Many international residents work remotely for companies abroad without any issues.

In contrast, internet service in hotels and tourist areas is often unreliable and may not be sufficient for video conferences or applications requiring a fast connection. However, a local prepaid SIM card offers an affordable and fast LTE internet solution, which can be used to create a hotspot for your computer.

What construction quality can be expected in Fumba Town, Zanzibar/Tanzania?

The German developer CPS in Fumba Town works with three construction companies, two of which are directly managed by experienced German and Swiss managers and foremen. In total, more than 300 employees are employed and trained by these two construction companies.

In Fumba Town, about 50% of the houses are built using timber construction. Are there no issues with termite infestations or high humidity?

Our German and Swiss specialists have developed a timber construction method that is ideally suited for Africa. The challenge of termites is addressed both in terms of processing and construction techniques. The wood is dried to 8% moisture content, which termites do not like at all. Additionally, the wooden walls are designed in such a way that they are protected from pest infestations even after construction. When you visit Zanzibar, we would be happy to take you on a tour of the production facility.

Does a visa for Zanzibar also apply to Tanzania, and does a visa for Tanzania also apply to Zanzibar?

Yes, a visa for Tanzania covers both the mainland and Zanzibar, so you can travel freely between the two. You can easily travel between Zanzibar and the mainland using domestic flights or ferries.
