About Fumba Town

Our real estate projects are more than just buildings—they represent a commitment to a sustainable and socially inclusive future.

In Tanzania, we’re not only providing affordable housing for the local community but also creating a vibrant, welcoming environment for people from around the world who value sustainability and social inclusion—right here in Africa.

With a vision brought to life by German engineers and international architects, we have crafted a masterpiece that blends world-class standards with the rich essence of African living. Experience the exceptional quality of our construction and the many benefits our innovative concept has to offer.

By actively involving the local community in the building process, we create jobs, empower people, and promote the transfer of valuable skills and knowledge.

Fumba Town – A Paradise of Permaculture

Permaculture is a design system that works in harmony with nature to create resilient and sustainable human settlements and social systems.

The land surrounding Fumba Town is home to 156 species of trees and plants. These will be carefully integrated into the city’s neighborhoods and parks. In addition, organic waste from residents is composted, creating a sustainable cycle for the entire community.

Permaculture Benefits for Investors and Residents:

  • Increased Property Value​
  • With green spaces, clean air and a focus on nature, residents of Fumba Town experience an exceptional quality of life.
  • Harmonious Living Environment​

We are partnering with CPS Africa to bring this amazing project to life.

All our partners and their team members, who live and work with us, are exceptional people dedicated to making a positive impact.
