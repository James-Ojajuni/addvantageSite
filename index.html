Frequently Asked Questions

How many people can obtain the Golden Visa?

The Golden Visa can be obtained for up to 6 people. Typically, this includes the parents and up to 4 financially dependent children under the age of 18. Eligibility for the visa remains valid as long as the house or apartment is owned. The visa in the passport must be renewed every 2 years and costs US$500 for the main applicant and US$50 for each additional person.
