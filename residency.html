Residency & Golden Visa in Zanzibar

Fumba Town offers an exceptional opportunity for individuals and families seeking residency in Zanzibar through strategic property investment. With investments starting from $100,000, you can secure a Golden Visa that grants residency benefits for you and your loved ones.​

Golden Visa Overview

Purchase property in Fumba Town valued at $100,000 or more.​ Visa covers the investor, spouse, and up to four children under 18.

Obtain a Class C11 visa, valid for 2 years and renewable as long as the property is owned. Visa costs $550 for the primary applicant and $50 for each additional family member.

Additional Perks:

  • Access to national driving license.
  • Resident tariffs on airlines, ferries, and events.
  • 50% exemption on stamp duty and capital gains tax.
  • 100% foreign ownership and profit repatriation.

Retirement Visa Option

For individuals aged 55 and above, Zanzibar offers a Retirement Visa without the need for significant investment.​

Requirements:

  • Proof of Income: Demonstrate sufficient financial means through pension statements or passive income sources.​
  • Accommodation: Option to purchase a small apartment starting from $35,000 or rent from $350 per month.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many people can obtain the Golden Visa?

The Golden Visa can be obtained for up to 6 people. Typically, this includes the parents and up to 4 financially dependent children under the age of 18. Eligibility for the visa remains valid as long as the house or apartment is owned. The visa in the passport must be renewed every 2 years and costs US$500 for the main applicant and US$50 for each additional person.
